...Chocolate Cubes...

Chocolate and pistachio flavors... Chocolate cubes prepared with only 4 ingredients are served with colored paper or by dipped toothpick. Chocolate cubes, especially on crowded tables, can also be presented at special events such as birthdays.

Recipe: Rukiye Beyza SarÄ±kaya

Servings:4 Prep:30 minutes Cook:10 minutes
Ingredients for Chocolate Cubes Recipe
  • 350 gr bitter chocolate
  • 150 ml cream
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 150 gr pistachios (can also be almonds or hazelnuts)
How to make Chocolate Cubes?
  1. Melt the bitter chocolate benmari style.
  2. Add the cream and butter to the melted chocolate, mix well and take the pot to a counter.
  3. Lightly beat the pistachios in the mortar and cut them into large and small pieces.
  4. Add the crumbled peanuts to the chocolate mixture and mix.
  5. If you have the chocolate you have prepared, put it on an aluminum ready square tray, otherwise it will overflow from the edges and be oiled. pour it into a small tray that you have placed paper in.
  6. Leave in the refrigerator for two hours.
  7. Remove the chocolate from the mold. Divide into square pieces.
